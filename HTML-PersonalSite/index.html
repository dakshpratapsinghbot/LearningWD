Naruto Uzumaki

7th Hokage of leaf.

Naruto Uzumaki becomes the 7th Hokage of the Hidden Leaf Village (Konohagakure) in the Naruto series. After years of hard work, determination, and countless battles, Naruto finally achieves his dream of becoming Hokage, following in the footsteps of his mentors and predecessors. Wears a traditional Hokage cloak and hat with the Kanji for "Seventh" (七代目). Oversees the protection and administration of the village. Is one of the strongest shinobi, having mastered powerful techniques like Sage Mode, the Rasengan, and the Nine-Tails Chakra Mode. Works towards maintaining peace in the shinobi world, including helping rebuild relationships between the Hidden Leaf and other villages. His role as Hokage is central in Boruto: Naruto Next Generations, where he faces the challenges of balancing his duties as leader with his responsibilities as a father to Boruto and Himawari.

Training

  • Basic Ninja Skills: Naruto learned the basics of ninjutsu, taijutsu, and genjutsu at the Ninja Academy, though he struggled at first, especially with the Clone Jutsu (Bunshin no Jutsu). He was often mischievous and performed poorly in written and theoretical exams.
  • Summoning Jutsu (Kuchiyose no Jutsu): Naruto trains with Jiraiya, one of the Legendary Sannin, to learn the Summoning Jutsu, allowing him to summon toads, including Gamabunta, a powerful toad boss.
